Norway and our way

Welcome to PLAD

A visionary Norwegian clothing brand dedicated to preserving the majestic beauty of the Norwegian wildlife, particularly the grand blue seals. Established in 2020 amidst the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, PLAD has emerged as a unique fusion of conservation, art, and disruptive fashion. Rooted in Oslo, Norway, our brand carries a profound appreciation for Norwegian culture and aims to redefine the fashion industry by breaking traditional boundaries.

The DNA

1

Our Mission

At Sealence, our mission is twofold: to champion the protection and welfare of the remarkable blue seals that grace the Norwegian shores, and to ignite a revolution in the fashion world. By intertwining conservation with cutting-edge design, we strive to create a brand that not only resonates with fashion enthusiasts but also raises awareness about the importance of environmental sustainability.

2

Conservation and Partnership

We have partnered with the esteemed association "Sealence" (a name inspired by our brand), dedicated to safeguarding the habitat and well-being of the grand blue seals. With every purchase you make from our collection, a percentage of the proceeds directly supports the efforts of this organization, ensuring that the enchanting seals continue to thrive in their natural habitat.

3

Oslo's Cultural Spirit

Located in the vibrant city of Oslo, Sealence draws inspiration from the rich cultural heritage of Norway. Our designs pay homage to the indigenous art forms, folklore, and traditions that have shaped this nation throughout history. From the breathtaking landscapes to the intricate craftsmanship, our garments embody the essence of Norwegian culture, serving as a contemporary canvas for artistic expression.

4

Disrupting Fashion

Sealence disrupts the norms of the fashion industry by challenging established codes and conventions. Our designs push boundaries and encourage individuals to embrace their unique style. We believe that fashion is an art form, and through our innovative creations, we empower individuals to make bold statements, express their individuality, and defy the ordinary.

5

Quality and Sustainability

We take pride in crafting high-quality garments that combine comfort, durability, and style. Each piece is meticulously designed using sustainable materials and ethical production practices, ensuring that our commitment to the environment is reflected in every stitch. By choosing Sealence, you join us in making a positive impact on both the fashion industry and the world we inhabit.

6

Join the Revolution

Sealence invites you to embark on a fashion journey unlike any other. Experience the fusion of conservation, art, and cultural preservation as we redefine the boundaries of style. Together, let's celebrate the grand blue seals of Norway, embrace the beauty of Norwegian heritage, and leave an indelible mark on the world of fashion.
